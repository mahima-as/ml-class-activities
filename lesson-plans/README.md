## Pedagogical Approach

These activities are designed for use within a **specifications grading** system
that incorporates **active learning** and **group work**:

- **Pass/fail**: Each activity is graded pass or fail based on clear specifications
  released when the activity is discussed in class.
- **Revisions**: Students may revise and resubmit up to two times.
- **Group work**: Group discussion is built into the suggested timelines.
  Submissions are individual unless otherwise noted.
- **Modular timing**: Each plan lists a minimum and maximum time. Instructors
  can choose how much of the activity to use in a given class session.

## Suggested Activity Format

A typical 30–45 minute activity follows this structure:

| Phase | Time | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| Introduction | 5 min | Instructor introduces the concept and activity |
| Individual work | 10 min | Students work independently on the notebook |
| Group discussion | 10 min | Small groups compare approaches and results |
| Share out | 10 min | Groups share findings; instructor facilitates |
| Wrap-up | 5 min | Instructor summarizes key takeaways |
